About Us

MoodMuse is your personal recommendation engine that bridges the world of emotions with the art of storytelling. We understand that your mood influences what you want to read or watch. Through a quick, interactive quiz, we identify your current emotional vibe and curate a personalized moodboard. Using this moodboard, we recommend handpicked books and movies tailored to your state of mind.

But it doesn’t stop there—MoodMuse evolves with you. Based on your past choices and preferences, our system refines its suggestions to match both your heart and history. Whether you're feeling euphoric, melancholic, or curious—MoodMuse has a story for every soul.
